Federated Learning for healthcare Fraud Detection

In this project, I implemented Federated Learning to enhance fraud detection in decentralized healthcare datasets while preserving data privacy. By optimizing loss functions, our models achieved approximately 7% higher accuracy than traditional Binary Cross-Entropy (BCE) Loss at a 0.3 Maximum False Positive Rate (Max FPR) threshold. Min-Max Loss, Pairwise Sigmoid (PSM) Loss, and Federated X-Risk L2 (FEDXL2) Loss significantly outperformed BCE, demonstrating the effectiveness of advanced optimization techniques in fraud detection. These findings highlight how Federated Learning can improve accuracy while ensuring secure, privacy-preserving AI solutions in healthcare.

Stock Volatility Prediction Using Public Sentiment and Financial Data

Understanding market movements requires more than just financial metricsâ€”public sentiment plays a crucial role in stock price fluctuations. In this project, I developed a predictive model that combines Natural Language Processing (NLP) on social media sentiment with quantitative financial data to forecast stock volatility.

By analyzing over 50,000 financial data points and 10,000+ Reddit and Yahoo Finance posts, our model achieved 70.1% prediction accuracy. Leveraging transformer models like RoBERTa and FinBERT, we identified hidden patterns where stocks with high sentiment but weak fundamentals declined, while undervalued stocks with low sentiment unexpectedly gained. Eye Ball Cursor Movement Using Deep Learning

Imagine controlling your computer just by looking at the screenâ€”this project brings that vision to reality. Using deep learning and real-time eye tracking, I developed an AI-powered system that detects gaze direction and translates it into cursor movements, enabling hands-free navigation.

Trained on over 6,000 eye images, the model utilizes Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) detection combined with OpenCV and deep learning techniques to precisely track gaze patterns. In addition to cursor control, the system enables left-click and right-click actions by detecting respective eye blinks, making it a truly interactive and AI-powered accessibility solution.

This innovation enhances accessibility for individuals with motor impairments, providing a seamless and intuitive way to interact with digital devices.
